Master Your Billing: A Guide for Small Businesses

Understanding the essentials of invoicing in India.

What is a GST Invoice?

A GST invoice is a bill issued by a registered seller to a buyer. It details the goods or services, their value, and the applicable GST (CGST, SGST, or IGST).

Why Go Digital?

Digital invoices look professional, reduce paper clutter, are easier to track for records, and help you get paid faster by sending them instantly.

Key Details on Every Bill

Every professional bill should include: Your & Client's Details (Name, GSTIN, Address), Invoice No., Date, Item Description, Taxable Value, and Total Amount.

CGST, SGST, & IGST?

Simple: Use CGST + SGST for sales *within* your state (e.g., Mumbai to Pune). Use IGST for sales *outside* your state (e.g., Mumbai to Delhi).
